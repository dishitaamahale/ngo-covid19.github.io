Our current activities

Midst the spread of COVID-19 affecting millions, our NGO teams are working to reach basic ration and other hygiene material to people.We turned our workshops and centres into 3 face mask-making units , taking up a daily production of 2,500 masks.

Sanitizers

Due to demand for alcohol-based hand sanitizers during the COVID-19 pandemic, we ensure that there are sanitizers provided to the ones who can't afford it.

Masks

Advises the use of simple cloth face coverings to slow the spread of the virus and help people who may have the virus and do not know it from transmitting it to others.

Food

During this lockdown period ,many under priviliged people do not get food daily. We ensure that our help reaches to as many poor people as possible.

Check-ups

Necessity of the hour is to do check-ups of the ones who feel feverish or are suffering from cold and flu.This will help us stop spreading of this pandemic.
